Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 3 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 1/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter (melted)

Time

⏱ 15:00

Preparation

1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

2

Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.

3

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

4

Brown on both sides and serve hot with syrup, butter, or fruit.
